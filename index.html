NUStudy is an application for managing your lecture notes and flash cards, so you can study and play at the same time. All university members – students, lecturers, tutors, even research staff – can come together and share the foundation of their existence for the greater good.

Acknowledgements

  • Libraries used: JavaFX, Jackson, JUnit5
